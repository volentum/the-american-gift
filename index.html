The Big Idea

The system
was broken.
They called it
normal. It wasn't.

The gap between how the mortgage industry should work — and how it actually works.

A family walks into a bank because they trust the name. They assume the institution will give them the best option.

But what if the best option was never shown to them?

What if the loan they received was not the best loan for their life — but the easiest loan for the institution's system, margins, and internal incentives?

That is the gap The American Gift was written to close.

About the Author

Eddy Perez

Eduardo G. Perez, Jr. is the Founder and CEO of EPM, a Top 50 mortgage company and one of the fastest-growing firms in the nation. He serves as Chairman of MORPAC, the political action committee for the Mortgage Bankers Association, and sits on the MBA Board of Directors. A Cuban-American entrepreneur known across the industry as "The Perfect Storm," Eddy leads with honor, duty, discipline, and gratitude — values that define his mission to build a better tomorrow for families, communities, and the mortgage profession.
